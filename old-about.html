Art Direction

Carnival ride of photos and interactive components. Build in some white spaces to give visitors some breathing time (like a rollercoaster). Take some risks. Intended audience is professionals (smart, technical, better than average devices). Don't worry as much about guiding visitors through the site or supporting old browsers and devices. Being tasteful, reserved, or consistent is secondary. This is meant to show depth and range of skill and style. I love photos and building interactive components. Show that. Nail the microanimations on the site. Focus on timing and composition.
